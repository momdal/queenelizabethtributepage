Elizabeth I

The Virgin Queen

A timeline of the Queen's life:

  • 1533 - Born in Greenwich, England
  • 1558 - Mary I passes away and Elizabeth is crowned Queen of England.
  • 1577–84 - Elizabeth provides financial support for Admiral Francis Drake’s expedition. A colony in North America is named Virginia after the Queen.
  • 1587 - Prisoner of 19 years, Mary Stuart is accused of an assassination plot against the Queen. Elizabeth signs the warrent for Mary's death, and Mary is beheaded.
  • 1588 - King Phillip of Spain attacks England with a naval Armada. The Spanish Armada is met with heavy opposition in the English Channel and suffer great losses. Elizabeth's victory over Spain is one of the greatest feat's of her reign.
  • 1603 - Queen Elizabeth passes March 24th. She is laid to rest at Westminster Abbey. James I, Mary Stuart's son is crown King of England.
